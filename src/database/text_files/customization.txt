PARTS OF A CHARACTER
- Archetype: A one-word summary of a character that grants both combat and narrative effects (Strong, Fast, etc.)
- Talents: Comprise magic and other abilities. Should have narrative effects, magic is a prereq for the Disciplines
    - Magic + Jobs
    - Larger abilities that tend to impact travel/inventory or similar
- Boons: Minor abilities that are more like nice tricks than full abilities
    - Give baseline for other mechanics
    - Small, more minor abilities that mostly affect narrative checks
- Disciplines: Combat abilities
- Equipment
- Spirit Customization
