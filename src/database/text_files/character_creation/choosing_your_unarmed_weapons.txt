All spiritbound have a basic level of competency with unarmed weapons and, as such, all have access to the Slam and Hand Unarmed Weapons. Some spiritbound, however, have trained more heavily in unarmed combat, as shown by their selection of Techniques with the _Martial_ Keyword.
&nbsp;
For every Technique with the _Martial_ Keyword a character has, select an additional Unarmed Weapon. While they deal less damage, Unarmed weapons tends to inflict more Statuses and can frequently be used for only 1 AP.
