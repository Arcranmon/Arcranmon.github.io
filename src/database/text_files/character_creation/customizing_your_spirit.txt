### Customizing Your Spirit
Next is customizing your spirit by determining how large it is and what weapons it has access to! A smaller spirit will be quicker and can be _Manifested_ at greater distances, while larger spirits will be hardier and have more health. A spirit's weapons represent the fangs, claws, spines, and other aspects of the beast it resembles that it can use to attack; the weapons below represent general archetypes, and should be reflavored as-needed to create your ideal spirit.
