These 'weapons' are that in mechanics only; unarmed weapons represent punches, kicks, and all other strikes making use of the body. While they deal little damage, unarmed strikes are _Fluid_ and can be used for 1 AP using the Unarmed Strike Technique, which all characters have access to. Even more, all Unarmed weapons inflict a great deal of _Vulnerable_, allowing characters to increase the damage dealt with successive strikes. All characters being with the Hand and Slam weapons but may  unlock more specialized unarmed weapons by learning Techniques with the _Martial_ keyword. Many of these advanced unarmed weapons that can be learned provide powerful circumstantial benefits, making them an effective tool for enabling powerful offensive and defensive abilities.
